Search Meetup Munich!

Living in munich? Interested in search, information retrieval or NLP? Tired of having introductory meetings without deep dives into search technologies like lucene, elasticsearch, solr, senseidb or sphinx?

Let us try to change that!

Follow @SearchMUC

Vendor neutral

  • It's all about search, not about vendors or products
  • Programming language agnostic
  • Search product agnostic

Topics wanted

  • No marketing talks, technical content only!
  • Open source search products (lucene, solr, elasticsearch, sphinx)
  • Details about your custom search implementation
  • Low level talks about search/information retrieval/NLP

Meetings

  • Every 2 months, with pizza & beer, in the evening
  • Different venues, so we get to know search companies in MUC
  • Interested in offering a location? Drop me a note!
